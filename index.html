Earnings Call Semantic Search

This application takes a txt version of an Quarterly Earnings Call Transcript and creates embeddings so that a semantic search may be performed. This allows for an accurate searching of these large documents. Additionally, the application can extract vital financial data (EPS, Revenue, Expenses, Net Income) automatically. Simply upload an earnings transcript and create embeddings. Then you are free to perform a search, or extract financial information.

Note that extracting financial data may take a few moments and may not always be accurate due to the nature of these transcripts.

Search results are listed in order of likelihood of correct response.
